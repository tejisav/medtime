About

Our application will help patients take their medication on time and assess their own health improvement. Thus, minimizing the chances of repeated visits to clinic and improving patient’s health.The main problem which the patients are facing nowadays is that they have to repeatedly visit the doctor for the same illness. This is because they often miss to take their medication on time.
